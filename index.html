What's RobinPad?

A small beginning for a long-term idea

RobinPad is not about big plans or big launches, it is a  journey of personal learning , built around a concept that I have always wanted to make a reality. For now, it is just the basics, evolving step by step.

A simple and minimal set of tools

RobinPad is a collection of lightweight, distraction-free tools designed for simple tasks. No clutter, no unnecessary features—just intuitive, efficient tools that let you focus on what matters.

Just Write. Just Draw. Nothing Else.

No complex menus. No overwhelming features. RobinPad is built for those who want to write or draw effortlessly, without distractions. Open it, create something, export it—as simple as that.

RobinPad is a project by Roberto Nieto (@syntetyc)
